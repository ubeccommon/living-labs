Blockchain & Token Technology

Notice on UBECrc Tokens

The Living Labs Initiative uses blockchain technology (Stellar Network) to document environmental observations. The issuance of UBECrc tokens (Universal Basic Environmental Contribution) serves as a recognition mechanism for contributions to environmental data collection.

Important: The UBECrc tokens are utility tokens that serve exclusively as a recognition mechanism for contributions to environmental data collection. They do not constitute securities and are not designed as a means of payment. They serve exclusively internal project purposes within the framework of scientific data collection, education, and the citizen science approach.
