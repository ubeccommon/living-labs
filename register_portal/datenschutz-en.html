3. Data Collection on This Website

Server Log Files

The provider of the pages automatically collects and stores information in so-called server log files, which your browser automatically transmits to us. These are:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL
  • Hostname of the accessing computer
  • Time of the server request
  • IP address

Legal Basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and efficient provision of our website)

5. Device Registration (senseBox)

When registering environmental sensors:

  • Device serial number
  • Geographic location (latitude/longitude)
  • Sensor types
  • Measurement timestamps
  • Assignment to registered steward (optional)

Legal Basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent) and Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in scientific research)

6. Blockchain Integration

This project uses the Stellar blockchain for:

  • Distribution of UBEC Reciprocity Credits (UBECrc)
  • Verification of data contributions
  • Transparent documentation of reciprocal exchanges
Important: Data stored on the blockchain is immutable and publicly accessible. We only store hashes and transaction IDs on the blockchain, not personal data.

7. Your Rights Regarding Your Data

You have the following rights at any time:

  • Right to access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77 GDPR)

8. Third-Party Services

We may share data with:

  • OpenSenseMap (for senseBox integration)
  • Stellar Network (for blockchain transactions)
  • Research institutions (anonymized data only for scientific purposes)

9. Data Security

We protect your data through technical and organizational measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Access restrictions on servers
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Pseudonymization/anonymization where possible
