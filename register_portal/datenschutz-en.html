3. How We Collect Your Data

Your data is collected in two ways: by you providing it to us (e.g., through contact forms or steward registration) and automatically by our IT systems when you visit the website (e.g., browser type, operating system, time of page visit).

4. What We Use Your Data For

Some data is collected to ensure error-free provision of the website. Other data may be used to analyze your user behavior with your consent.

Within the Living Science Initiative, data is used for:

  • Registration of environmental stewards
  • Management of environmental sensors (SenseBox)
  • Recording and managing environmental observations
  • Blockchain-based documentation (anonymized)
  • Management of UBEC token allocations

5. Your Rights Regarding Your Data

You have the following rights at any time:

  • Right to access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77 GDPR)

8. Blockchain Technology and UBEC Tokens

Important Notice on Blockchain Data: Data stored on the Stellar blockchain cannot be deleted due to the technical nature of blockchain systems. We only store anonymized or pseudonymized data on the blockchain.

What data is stored on the blockchain?

  • Anonymized environmental observation data (without personal reference)
  • Observation timestamps
  • Geographic coordinates (rounded/imprecise)
  • UBEC token transactions (public wallet addresses, no names)

9. SenseBox and Sensor Data

When registering an environmental sensor (SenseBox), the following data is collected:

  • SenseBox ID
  • Sensor location data
  • Measurement parameters (temperature, humidity, air pressure, etc.)
  • Measurement timestamps
  • Assignment to registered steward (optional)

Legal Basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent) and Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in scientific research)

10. Data Security

We protect your data through technical and organizational measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Access restrictions on servers
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Pseudonymization/anonymization where possible

11. Third-Party Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • OpenSenseMap (for SenseBox integration)
  • Stellar Network (for blockchain transactions)
  • Research institutions (anonymized data only for scientific purposes)
